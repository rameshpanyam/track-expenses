Signature moves — repeat religiously
01
Currency-spaced numerals. Mono ₹ symbol, half-space gap, then tabular-figured numbers. Never glue them.
02
One black hero per screen. Charts always live on the black card. Card surfaces never carry chart strokes.
03
Coral is the rare ingredient. Max 2 coral elements visible per frame (callout pill + FAB dot, or active tab + one accent card).
04
Pill nav + round FAB. Nav is a card-toned pill. FAB is matte black with a coral dot, bordered in card-tone for "lifted" feel.
05
Tone-on-tone tx rows. Cards sit on page bg with only a 1px ink-08 border. No shadows except on the device shell.
06
Paper grain, not gradient. The warm beige has tooth via SVG noise; we never paint smooth gradients on surfaces.
07
Italic + coral for emphasis. Headings can swap one phrase to italic Manrope in coral — once per page, never twice.
08
Mono labels, sans values. Every label is mono-uppercase-tracked. Every value is display sans. The contrast is the rhythm.
